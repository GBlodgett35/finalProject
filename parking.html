Short Term Parking

Ideal for travelers requiring parking less than 24 hours. The most convenient for dropping off and picking up passengers. Direct access to Transit Level.

RATES:

$2.00 for 1st hour

$4.00 each additional hour

$26.00 daily max

Daily max is calculated on a 24-hour basis. All hours exceeding a 24-hour period will be charged at the hourly rate up to the daily max.

Long Term Parking

This section is for those who are going to be gone for more than a day. We offer vallet services and will take your car to the long term parking garage.

RATES:

$20.00 for 1st day

$8.00 each additional hour

$200.00 daily max

The long term storage garage is protected from the weather and has ample secutriy to ensure your vehicle is safe at all times. The max time allowed to park is 30 days

Extended Economy Parking

The most economical lot for the traveler on long trips. Free shuttle service to/from the terminal via buses.

RATES:

Free 1st hour

$2.00 for 2nd hour

After 2 hours, the normal rate of $8.00/day will go into effect

$8.00 daily max

Daily maximum is calculated on a 24-hour basis.
