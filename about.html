About Us

Welcome to Buffalo Wings Airport!

Continuous Expansion-Exciting Times Ahead


Buffalo Wings Airport was built by small group of students that were taking IS 270 class at Harding University and was opened on 25 April in 2019. Our Airport is committed to increasing air service, improving customer service and inspiring growth in the Jacksonville region.


Our Vision

To reflect and serve the community, inspire the industry, and advance the region's role as a world leader.

Our Mission

To be the world's leading airport company, growing a safe, secure and vibrant air hub in Jacksonville, Florida and enhancing the communities we serve worldwide.
