Welcome to Buffalo Wings Airport!

Here at BWA, we aim to make your trip as enjoyable and worry free as possible. While you wait, please enjoy our beautiful terminals and gorgeous views. We also have a mouth-wateringly good food court, featuring restaurants like Eleven Madison Park Express and Ruth's Chris Steakhouse, as well as an industry-leading shopping center, which includes a Hugo Boss and Supreme. Have a great flight, and enjoy your trip!
